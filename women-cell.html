About Women Cell

The Women's Cell at Malabar Dental College & Research Centre is a dedicated forum fostering gender equality and women's advancement. We organize events, workshops, and discussions to raise awareness about women's rights, health, and social issues.

Providing a supportive environment, we address academic and personal concerns while promoting skill-building and networking. Through these efforts, we empower women to excel and lead within and beyond our campus. Our commitment to creating a respectful and inclusive atmosphere drives positive change and meaningful progress.
