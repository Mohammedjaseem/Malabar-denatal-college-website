Scholarships

Malabar Dental College offers scholarships and fellowships to help students and their families with dental college expenses. These financial aids, including scholarships and grants, do not require repayment. Students may be eligible for College-based scholarships, federal or state grants, or external scholarships intended for students in need.

Scholarships Sources

We offer scholarships and financial aid options to ensure that talented and deserving individuals have access to quality dental education. Our scholarships include merit-based, need-based, and specialized scholarships for various fields of study.

  • Post Matric Scholarships Scheme for Minorities
  • Merit Cum Means Scholarship For Professional and Technical Courses.
  • SC/ST FEE WAIVER
  • C H Muhammedkoya Scholarship
  • FISHERIES SCHOLARSHIP
  • ALAVI HAJI MEMORIAL TRUST SCHOLARSHIP

Guidance and Advice

This list contains various scholarships, each with its specific eligibility criteria and benefits. Students are encouraged to check the respective scholarship details for further information and application guidelines.

Featured (MDCRC)

Malabar Dental College and Research Centre is a renowned dental institution located in Kerala, India. With a commitment to excellence, it offers comprehensive dental education and training. The college provides a variety of scholarships and grants to support students in their academic journey, ensuring access to quality education.
