Dr. Meenu Merry C. Paul

Principal

Biography

She is an eminent prosthodontist and an excellent academician, having a teaching experience of over 20 years in various under graduate and post graduate instituitions across Kerala. She is a founder member of ISPRP(Indian Society of Prosthodontics- Restorative- Periodontics) and life member of IPS (Indian Prosthodontic Society).

Education

  • BDS, Dentistry, Vinayaka Mission's Dental College, Selam
    • MDS, Prosthodontics, K.L.E' s institute of Dental Sciences, Karnataka
