Students Union

Home >Students Union

We, DHEEKSHA; Malabar dental students union 2022-23 is a dedicated organization that represents and advocates for the interests of all students in the campus.

We are committed to organize and conduct diverse range of impactful programs and events that cater to the needs and preferences of the student body. These programs are designed to promote academic, social, and personal development, fostering a vibrant and inclusive campus community. Through their dedication and creativity, the student union strives to create a positive and enriching college experience for every student.
