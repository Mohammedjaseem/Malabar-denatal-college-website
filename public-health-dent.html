Department Overview

The Department of Public Health Dentistry deals with the prevention of oral disease and promotion of oral health. The department is involved in the assessment of key dental health needs and come up with effective solution to improve the dental health of population rather than individuals.

The department conducts various outreach programs, institution adoption programs, school dental health programs etc. The department has a wonderful museum containing various patient education models, health education charts, and an antique dental chair. Public Health Dentistry adds to the evidence-based dentistry through research works & biostatistical analysis of recent advances in dental science.

Why Public Health Dentistry is Special ?

Public Health Dentistry is the latest specialty in dentistry and deals with preventive treatment while all other departments deal with curative treatment. Public Health Dentistry is a department where doctors go to patients. The department deals with research and biostatistics, principles of administration and also dental ethics & jurisprudence. This specialty has high scope in India & abroad, where of high demand in ICMR & World Health Organization.

About Head of Department

Dr Abdul Saheer is a passionate academician working as Head of the department with 5+ years of teaching experience. He's thoroughly a dedicated teacher and emphasizes on one on one teaching interactions with students as to grasp their attention towards the subject. He has completed BDS from Kannur university (2004-2009) and secured University rank from RGUHS in MDS exam for the academic year of 2013-2016. He is,currently, pursuing PhD under KUHS. A number of awards were rewarded under his name such as AKS Global faculty award 2021, Indian Health Professional award 2020, RULA Dental researcher award 2019. He, also, has 33 National and international publications. Furthermore, he encourages and guides the students to conduct research, thereby introducing them in the field of research. His expertise not only ends with his expertise on research, but also has vast knowledge domain in Biostatistics too. Due to his immense interest towards academics, later years, he innovated a teaching online platform for all the students across the globe by creating his very own youtube channel (Dentistry n more). Its that handle where each dental expertise teach and explain their respective subjects and clarify the doubts instilled within the viewers. He organises various outreach programmes and thus, provides health awareness classes to the public at the best of his ability. He is currently collaborating with Zone school of Business studies,London as research consultant.
