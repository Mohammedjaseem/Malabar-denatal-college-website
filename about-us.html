Malabar’s History

Malabar Dental College and Research Centre, is one of the most premier educational projects of Malabar Educational & Charitable Trust. It is specifically designed for all dental care aspirants who want to land in the profession of dental care with smart and quality education. This educational institution was started in 2007 under the University of Calicut. In 2010, it was truly blessed to come under the aegis of Kerala University of Health Science, Trissur.

The beautiful campus situated near to the banks of Bharathapuzha adds to the beauty of the campus, which spreads over 25 acres of land. It’s lush green and pollution-free campus is well designed and graciously equipped with all modern facilities. MDC offers academic programs from undergraduate to postgraduate programs in seven specialties. Collectively, our students and residents also benefit from the skills and experience of our highly devoted faculty, and a rich collaborative environment.

Campus Location

Serene Location on top of small hillock, away from city, but well connected by air, rail and road, provide the proper calm and quite atmosphere for the student to do sincere studies.

University Results

Pass percentages in recent years were 96-100%. Our student Mrs Sheenu M.S is the First Rank holder of Calicut University in 2012 , and a few students hold the rank in the university every year. From 2010 onwards college approved under Kerala University of Health Sciences.

Academics

Highly trained and qualified faculty members in all the departments to impart proper individual training to make the student to become a good quality dentist.

Principal's Message

Malabar Dental College is an institution that embellishes the cause of dental education in its truest sense. From very humble beginnings, this Mecca of Dentistry has earned reputation by catering huge services in the field of imparting excellent education. The College has witnessed a successful metamorphosis with an intake of 100 undergraduate students in this current academic year. At MDC, vision for excellence is testimony to its proliferous growth. The extremely supportive teaching and non-teaching faculty and students of various ongoing batches are the most important value addition to our exuberance. As the principal of this prestigious institution, I congratulate and wish good luck to each and every student who is and has been a part of this wonderful journey.

—   Dr. Meenu Merry C Paul

Our Mission

At MDC we try our best to equip our students to dream without limits and enable them to adapt themselves to the changing environment. We help them face challenges by which students improve their skills and their outlook towards the situations. This helps in making an overall contribution towards the welfare of the society.

We Provide

Innovative and globally recognized programs that contributes to the holistic development of the individual’s society. Our programs are equipped to facilitate learning through discipline, discovery and dissemination of knowledge. We work on empowering our students to cope with real world environment.

Our Vision

To nurture this Campus with its dream plans of expansion into a Centre of excellence for quality and professional education and the construction of a super specialty hospital, Higher secondary school, College of Nursing, Ayurveda college and Paramedical Institution apart from many other ambitious plans which are to be embarked on.
