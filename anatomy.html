Department Overview

The Anatomy Department started functioning in 2007 with its fine infrastructure and adequate number of cadavers.

The Department of Anatomy explore the structure in human body, which is the essential basic knowledge required for health care professionals. The department is well furnished with modern teaching and learning equipment which makes the learning process easy and compatible.

Why Anatomy Department is Special ?

Dissection Hall

The specious Dissection Hall is with sufficient number of cadavers, skeleton, specimens, models, etc. Demonstration area with X-ray lobby and charts makes the students to understand the structures effectively in depth. Students are used to do dissection of cadavers with supervision of well experienced teachers.

Museum

The museum provides an insight of the structure of human body to the student and the visitors as well. The Anatomy museum located in the Anatomy Department premise is with well-preserved human body specimens, dry and wet specimens. The models of various systems of the human body is also well arranged in the museum. The articulated skeleton is also another attraction of the museum.

Histology Lab

Histology lab provides adequate knowledge on microscopic structure of various body parts and tissues. Histology lab is with adequate number of microscopes, best quality histological slides and equipment. The basic knowledge on microscopic structure helps to understand the underlying histopathological conditions more effectively.

Cadaver Room

The specious cadaver room with well-preserved cadavers and other wet specimens are maintained well in the Department. The cadaver room is with proper ventilation and fumigating facility. The technician and non-teaching staffs are used to maintain the cadaver room and Department premise to function smoothly.

Department Library/Reference Area

The Anatomy Department is with good number of books for reference in the department itself.
