Department Overview

The Information Technology Department is a crucial part of an organization or institution responsible for managing and maintaining all aspects of information technology.

The IT Department at MDCRC is at the forefront of technological advancements. Our work encompasses software, app, and website development, graphic design, and system administration. We manage social media and outreach efforts, striving to digitize the campus fully. Our vision is to make the campus paperless, and we are the driving force behind campus digitalization, playing a pivotal role in its transformation.

Why Department of IT is Special?

Department Facilities

  • Software Development Lab: Equipped for software and app development.
  • Design Studio: Graphics and poster design.
  • Server Room: For system administration and data management.
  • Digital Outreach Hub: Social media and online engagement.
  • Digitalization Center: Focused on making the campus paperless.
  • Training Facilities: To empower college staff and students with IT skills.

Mission

Deliver cutting-edge software, creative design, robust infrastructure, and digital outreach for seamless campus advancement and sustainability.

Vision

To lead digital transformation, making our dental college paperless, while empowering with innovative IT solutions.
