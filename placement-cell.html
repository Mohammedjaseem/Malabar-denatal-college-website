Placement Cell Objectives

  • To promote and create awareness about career advancement and placement options available to students after the course.
  • To conduct activities that will help the students to get exposure to the current trends of the dentistry by way of knowledge, training, internship and placement.
  • To Continuously upgrade the knowledge of students to make them abreast with recent trends of dentistry through lectures, seminars, workshops etc.
  • To guide the students for preparations of various competitive exams like PG-NEET for MDS course admission etc.
  • To inform students about the available job oppurtunities in the government sectors and off campus drives and to arrange motivational talks.
