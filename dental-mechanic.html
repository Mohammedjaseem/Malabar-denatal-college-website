Dental Mechanic (DM)

Dental Mechanic is a demanding diploma course of 2 year duration.

This is a two year Diploma course started in 2011 with 10 seats, and it is affiliated to Directorate of Medical Education (DME), Government of Kerala. It teaches students in the areas of full dentures, partial dentures, fixed bridges, dental technology, and operating relevant equipment.
